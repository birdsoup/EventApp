Help

If you are experiencing trouble making an account, please ensure:

  • That your username contains only alphanumeric characters or the underscore ("_") symbol.
  • That your password is between 6 and 35 characters long.
  • That you are entering a valid email address.

If you are experiencing trouble signing into an existing account, please ensure:

  • That the information you are entering is correct.
  • That you are entering your username, and not your email address, in the username field.

If you are experiencing trouble searching for events, please ensure:

  • That you have allowed LocFun permission to access your geo-location.
  • That you have entered a search term.
  • That your search radius is not too small.
