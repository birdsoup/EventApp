LocFun offers several features to users:

  • Make an account: You can always search as a guest, but logged in users can have their preferences remembered.
  • Search for events: Specify a search term, a search radius, and filter by type of event if you prefer, to see a list of fun things to do in your area.
  • Bookmark events: If something sparks your interest, bookmark it for later. View all events you've bookmarked any time while logged in.
  • Buy ticket now: Takes you to the listing of an event so you can book your visit.
  • Popular events: Shows the events that are generating the most buzz in your area.
